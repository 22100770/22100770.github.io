Our Products

Red Dead Redemption 4

Red Dead Redemption 4 is an epic open-world Western adventure that continues the legacy of the legendary outlaw era. Set in the final days of the Wild West, the game follows a new group of outlaws struggling to survive as law, industry, and civilization begin to swallow the frontier. You play as Elias Mercer, a hardened gunslinger haunted by his past, who becomes entangled with a ragtag gang of fugitives trying to escape the tightening grip of federal lawmen and powerful railroad tycoons. As the gang rides across vast deserts, frozen mountains, and growing frontier towns, loyalties will be tested and survival will depend on every choice you make.

Call of Duty Modern Warfare 4

Step back into the shadows of global warfare in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 4, the next chapter in the legendary tactical shooter series. As geopolitical tensions erupt across multiple continents, a new generation of elite operators must confront a covert enemy network capable of destabilizing the world's most powerful nations. In this highn stakes campaign, players take on the role of a multinational special forces operative tasked with uncovering a secret coalition known only as The Phantom Directive. Using cutting-edge technology, advanced drone warfare, and unconventional tactics, this shadow organization launches synchronized attacks designed to plunge the world into chaos.

Minecraft 2

Minecraft 2 is the next evolution of the legendary sandbox adventure originally created and developed by Celtec Games. In this new chapter of the world-building phenomenon Minecraft, players explore a vastly expanded universe filled with deeper survival mechanics, massive new biomes, and limitless creative possibilities. Step into a procedurally generated world larger and more dynamic than ever. Discover towering mountain ranges, erupting volcanoes, floating islands, and hidden underground civilizations. Every biome is alive with new creatures, resources, and environmental events that change how you survive and explore.

Star Wars Battelfront 3

Enter the ultimate galactic war in Star Wars Battlefront 3, the most ambitious battle experience ever set in the legendary universe of Star Wars. Fight across iconic planets, command powerful heroes, and take part in massive battles where the fate of the galaxy is decided by your actions. Choose your side in the conflict between the heroic forces of the Rebellion and the oppressive might of the Empire. Play as legendary characters like Darth Vader, Ahsoka Tano, and Emperor Palpatine, each with unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle.

Grand Theft Auto VII

GTA VII is the next evolution of the open-world crime saga inspired by the legendary series from Rockstar Games. Set in a massive, living world bigger than any previous entry, the game blends high-stakes crime, deep storytelling, and complete player freedom. Explore Aurora City, a sprawling metropolis surrounded by deserts, tropical islands, mountain towns , and neon-lit nightlife districts. Every area has its own culture, gangs, businesses, and secrets. From luxury penthouses to underground fight clubs, the world reacts to your actions.

The Walking Dead: A Telltale Series: Season 5

Step back into the harrowing world of The Walking Dead, where every choice matters and survival is never guaranteed. In Season 5, players continue the journey of Clementine, now older, wiser, and hardened by years of living in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by the undead. As the story unfolds, you will lead a diverse group of survivors through a landscape of danger, betrayal, and heartbreak, face difficult moral choices that will shape the fate of your group and the world around you, navigate tense encounters with both the undead and other human factions, each with their own agendas and threats and you will also experience branching narratives, where every decision can have far-reaching consequences on who lives, who dies, and who you become.
